1. Rotary Peace Fellowships

  • Level: Master’s
  • Study Locations: Worldwide
  • Official Website

Each year, Rotary awards up to 130 fully funded fellowships to dedicated leaders worldwide. These fellowships cover tuition, fees, room and board, round-trip transportation, and expenses for internships and field studies.

Since its inception in 2002, the program has trained over 1,700 fellows, who now work in more than 140 countries, serving in leadership roles across governments, NGOs, educational institutions, peacekeeping agencies, and international organizations.

 

2. Aga Khan Foundation Scholarships for African Students

  • Levels: Master’s and PhD
  • Study Locations: Worldwide
  • Official Website

The Aga Khan Foundation’s International Scholarship Program (AKF ISP) offers postgraduate education opportunities to exceptional scholars from selected countries who lack other means of funding their studies.

Since its establishment in 1969, the program has supported over 1,000 students. Award recipients are chosen through a global, annual, and competitive application process, with a limited number of new awards granted each cycle.

 

3. MasterCard Foundation Scholarships

  • Levels: Master’s and PhD
  • Study Locations: The US and Africa
  • Official Website

The Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program aims to develop the next generation of transformative leaders by providing highly talented, service-oriented young people, primarily from Africa, with the opportunity to pursue higher education and improve their leadership potential.

Collaborating with educational institutions, NGOs, and the Mastercard Foundation Scholars community, the program ensures that young people receive inclusive and relevant education, transition into dignified work, and lead impactful lives.
